# WorkFlow

A simple, fast **PWA app** designed to make managing **Google Classroom assignments** easier and more interactive.

## Overview

**WorkFlow** is a web app / Progressive Web App that lets you check all your Google Classroom assignments with **just one Google sign-in**. Instead of navigating through multiple classes and pages or going to google classroom to look at to-do list, WorkFlow can summarize everything in one so you can quickly see what matters.

With a single tap you can instantly view:

* Missing assignments
* Late assignments
* Pending assignments

This allows you to stay organized and avoid overlooking work.

## Features

### Quick Google Classroom Overview

Sign in once with Google and immediately access your assignments across classes in one place.

### Smart Assignment Status

Assignments are automatically categorized so you can easily see:

* Missing work
* Late submissions
* Upcoming tasks

Or change the filter how you like.

### Interactive Performance Stats

WorkFlow turns your assignment data into **fun and useful statistics**, including:

* Productivity graphs
* Assignment streaks
* Performance trends
* Other fun interactive insights

This'll help you visualize how well you're keeping up with your work.

### AI Assistant

WorkFlow includes an **AI assistant** that can help you:

* Understand your assignments
* Ask questions about tasks
* Get recommendations on what to prioritize
* Receive quick guidance based on your current workload

The assistant analyzes your listed assignments and provides immediate suggestions.

### Calendar Integration

WorkFlow makes scheduling assignments on calendar simple.

You can:

* Automatically assign pending tasks to dates in just a click
* Drag assignments directly onto a calendar
* Export schedules to `.ics` format

This allows you to easily add assignments to apps like:

* Apple Calendar
* Outlook
* Other calendar applications

Helping you keep your academic schedule fully organized.

## Platform

WorkFlow is built as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**, meaning you can:

* Use it directly in your browser
* Install it like a native app
* Access it on desktop, tablet, or mobile
